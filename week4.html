ARTS 102

online

Welcome to another wonderful week of design.

Last week, we finished the first project. Please be sure to post your designs in the Discussion Forum for others to see. Take time to comment on other people's work. I hope you had fun working through the creative process of reading and vocab, leading to design concept sketches and explorations, followed by creating the final collages. We will be following the same process for the next two projects.

This week we start Project 2: a Propaganda Poster that advocates for something you feel passionate about. Like before, you will spend the first week learning the vocab and making design sketches. The second week will be spent creating the actual posters using whatever digital tools you choose to use.

I know some of you struggled with the process on the first project. It can be confusing and disorienting when the creative challenge is vague and when you can do just about anything you want to do. It can be difficult to work with loose rules and uncertain outcomes. I hope that working through the first project gave you a sense of the process. I'm available to answer any questions you have and to hold your hands through the process, if that helps. Please put me to use.

all of this week's assignments should be due by Sunday July 4. In recognition of the holiday, I'm moving the deadline to Tuesday July 6.
