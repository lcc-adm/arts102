SHARED GOOGLE FOLDER SETUP

As an LCC student, you have a Google Drive account for storing your files. You will create a folder in your Drive and share with me.

  1. Login to the my.lcc.edu portal.
  2. In the upper-right corner of your main page click the link to student email.
  3. In the upper-right corner of your email, click the 3x3 grid box and select Drive. This is your digital storage account.
  4. Press the big NEW icon in the upper left and select Folder. Name your folder lastname first name.
  5. Right-click on the new folder and choose to Share… your folder with me at bennetc7@star.lcc.edu
  6. Please make sure you are giving me permission to Edit that folder.

NOTES

This setup will only give me access to the content of one particular folder. I can’t see anything else in your google drive. This is a good thing. Please remember to turn in all assignments to this folder.

I will create a Graded folder within our shared folder. When I am done grading an assignment, it will get moved into the Graded folder. That lets me easily know what assignments need to be graded/regraded.
