ASSIGNMENTS

SPACE

Creating a believable sense of depth is an interesting and challenging task. There are so many details that we use to perceive depth (that we usually take for granted). Shadows help place objects in space. Overlapping gives us visual cues for what object is closer than other objects. Start looking around and paying attention to how you see the world around you and how things fit into that world. It is a fun way to experience the world.
