ARTS 102

online

Hello again and welcome to week 5. I hope you enjoyed the holiday weekend.

Last week we spent time exploring Rhythm, Line, and Shape. You got a chance to read about the concepts and explore the vocabulary. You spent time doodling and sketching out how to implement those concepts into your propaganda poster.

This week you are working to make the actual digital design. Use whatever design tools you have at your disposal. I have made some videos to help complete the project in Photoshop (and Photopea). You are not forced to use those tools, of course, so work with what you can.

As you finish the poster, you will be reflecting on the work and posting the images for your classmates to see.

As always, there is a weekly community design activity as well. I would like for you to go on a collection spree. Choose some topic or item and go collect a variety of images. More details are in the information page.

all of this week's assignments are due by Sunday July 8.
