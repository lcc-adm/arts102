ARTS 102

online

Hello and welcome to the Summer 2020 online version of ARTS 102.

Since this is summer, the course is quite accelerated. You will be completing a full-semester's worth of work in only two months. Each week there will be several activities to complete. Some will be quick and easy, others will take a bit of time. I recommend figuring out the tasks early and spreading them out over the week. Don't try to get everything done in one sitting.

This first week is all about getting the systems figured out, introducing you to the ebooks, and starting to play around with Photoshop. It may seem like there is a lot here, but that's only because it is spread out. Some of these tasks will not take very long to complete. There will be fewer (although deeper) tasks in future weeks.

photoshop stuff

We will be using Adobe Photoshop to create visual explorations based on different design concepts. This isn't a software class and you probably won't be making art worth hanging on a gallery wall, however you will need to learn enough of the tools to get around and complete your work. The plan this week is to get a copy of Photoshop and just tinker around.

  1. get a copy of Photoshop for yourself. Check here to get more information about how to acquire Photoshop and how to use the tools.
  2. make a self portrait picture in Photoshop
  3. turn your picture in to the assignment dropbox (Assessments tab > Assignments > Assignment 1)
