ARTS 102

online

Hello and welcome to the Summer 2021 online version of ARTS 102.

Since this is summer, the course is quite accelerated. You will be completing a full-semester's worth of work in only two months. Each week there will be several activities to complete. Some will be quick and easy, others will take a bit of time. I recommend figuring out the tasks early and spreading them out over the week. Don't try to get everything done in one sitting.

This first week is all about getting the systems figured out, introducing you to the ebooks, and starting to play around with Photoshop. It may seem like there is a lot here, but that's only because it is spread out. Some of these tasks will not take very long to complete. There will be fewer (although deeper) tasks in future weeks.

Not all assignments for this week are graded, but they are required.

all assignments are due by Sunday June 13.

assignments

art stuff

We will be (most likely) using Adobe Photoshop to create visual explorations based on different design concepts. You are able to choose what design tools you would like to use though, so don't feel like you have to use Photoshop. This isn't a software class and you probably won't be making art worth hanging on a gallery wall, however you will need to learn enough of the tools to get around and complete your work. The plan this week is to find your software and just tinker around.

  1. get a copy of Photoshop (or other program) for yourself. [[photoshop|Check here to get more information about how to acquire Photoshop and how to use the tools.]]
  2. make a [[assign1 self-portrait|self-portrait picture in whatever digital tools you choose.]]
  3. turn your picture in to the assignment dropbox (Assessments tab > Assignments > Assignment 1)

assignments

community design activity

It is notoriously difficult to create a sense of community in online classes. It is hard to hang out and share ideas when you aren't meeting together in the same physical location. This is unfortunate because art classes are especially wonderful places for creative community. Students can learn just as much from fellow artists as they can from the teacher.

I'm determined to find a fun way for everyone to become creative friends this summer. Each week you will have a design activity that you will share with the class. These assignments are meant to be fun and playful and not at all serious. They are meant to be hands-on explorations of creative play.

  1. [[community design challenge - make a zine|make a zine]].
  2. post your work on the Discussion forum (Communication tab).
