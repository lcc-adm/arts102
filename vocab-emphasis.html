As a way to understand the vocabulary from this unit, you will explain the definitions and find representative imagery. This process allows you to actively seek to understand the concepts.

Review the concepts in the Emphasis ebook.

The template for vocab can be found here. It is a Google Slides document. The vocab words for this unit are listed. You will make a copy of the document so that it can be edited and completed.

Alternatively, you can create a Google Docs page or use Microsoft Office apps. I'm open to whatever tools make the most sense to you, as long as you are using something I can easily access and read.

NOTES

  • Don't just copy/paste the vocab definition from the ebook. Rewrite it in your own words to help make sense of the meaning.
  • Use your favorite search engine to find representative images. Don't just randomly choose the images; take the time to assess whether they really do fit the vocab word.
  • You can also take your own photos or make your own drawings to capture the ideas.

ASSIGNMENT SUBMISSION

Complete the google document and turn it in to our shared folder.

ASSIGNMENT EVALUATION

  • 5 pts - words and definitions
  • 5 pts - representative imagery
