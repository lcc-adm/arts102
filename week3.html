Hello again and welcome to week 3.

Last week we spent time exploring Unity, Balance, and Emphasis. You got a chance to read about the concepts and explore the vocabulary. You spent time doodling and sketching out how to implement those concepts into your collages.

This week you are working to make the collages. Use whatever design tools you have at your disposal. I have made some videos to help complete the project in Photoshop (and Photopea). You are not forced to use those tools, of course, so work with what you can.

As you finish the two collages, you will be reflecting on the work and posting the images for your classmates to see.

As always, there is a weekly community design activity as well. This week, you are exploring your curiosity by trying out something new.

all of this week's assignments are due by Sunday June 27.
