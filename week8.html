ASSIGNMENTS

FINAL EXAM REVIEW

The final exam is now available. It is a departmental exam that everyone in ARTS 102 takes regardless of teacher or course delivery method. It is 70 questions, vocabulary-based, multiple-choice, open-book, and open-note. There is a two-hour limit for taking the exam so you won't have time to go in unprepared and expect to look up every answer. It is worth taking the time to review the course content before you take the test.

Here are some resources to help you review:

  • the ebooks from each unit have all the vocabulary and concepts
  • this list of vocab terms to understand
  • flashcards and video games at quizlet
  • flashcards and video games at cram
  • really bad (but useful) videogames at classtools.net
  • kahoot! not sure how well this works for individual review, but they are quite fun in my face-to-face classes. to do this right, you will need two devices, one to run the games and one to use for playing the games. maybe you can get a friend to help? warning, there are some completely random silly zingers in the games to throw off the students.
