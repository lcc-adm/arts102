For this week we will be diving into the world of design principles, in particular Unity and Emphasis and, of course, Photoshop. This week is an indication of what further weeks will be like: two design concepts with ebooks and related creative visual challenges.

There's a fun balance between the two concepts this week: Unity is all about organizing visual elements so that everything fits together and Emphasis is all about making one of those elements stand out from the rest of the other elements.

Both assignments are all about organizing elements into different compositions. Since you are just learning Photoshop, for the Unity designs you will work with simple rectangles. That should give you a nice intro to moving things around. For the Emphasis designs you will be creating and moving around text elements instead of simple rectangles. You will mostly use the same tools to do the work though. We're slowly adding complexity.

My advice for you as you start exploring the concepts is to focus on being playful with your art. You are not expected to make perfect pieces ready for a gallery wall. Think of them as sketches and doodles that are messy and rough. The important thing is to see what you can do with the concepts. If you can let go of that perfection, then you will have much better time in this class. Here's a great video from Ira Glass with a message that it is ok if your work isn't that good. Just keep trying and making things. Your work will get better as you go. By the end of the semester you will surprise yourself with what you are capable of. 

Have fun and happy designing!

ASSIGNMENTS

UNITY

Unity is about arranging all visual elements into a pleasing unified whole. You will need to look at both placement of individual elements and how they fit in relationship to other elements. Unity is the foundation of all other explorations you will be doing throughout this class.

  • read the Unity ebook
  • review the Unity Assignment, which includes technical instructions on getting started in Photoshop
  • download the Unity Template for Photoshop
  • create your design work - have fun!
  • save your work and post to the Unity Assignment dropbox. don't forget to do the self-assessment writing
  • post your work on the Unity Assignment discussion forum
  • comment on at least two posts from your classmates

ASSIGNMENTS

EMPHASIS

With Emphasis, you are trying to make one element stand out from the rest of the composition. The big trick with Emphasis is to use Contrast in some form to make an element different from the rest. Ways to play include making one element isolated from the rest, making one element bigger or smaller than the rest, making one element square and rectilinear when the rest are round and soft. You get to discover other ways to add contrast and build Emphasis.

  • read the Emphasis ebook
  • review the Emphasis Assignment, which includes technical instructions on getting started in Photoshop
  • download the Emphasis Template for Photoshop
  • create your design work - have fun!
  • save your work and post to the Emphasis Assignment dropbox. don't forget to do the self-assessment writing
  • post your work on the Emphasis Assignment discussion forum
  • comment on at least two posts from your classmates
